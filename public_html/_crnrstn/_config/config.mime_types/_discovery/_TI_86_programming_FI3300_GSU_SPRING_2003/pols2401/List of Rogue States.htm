Department of Homeland Security

1st of June 2002

From the office of the Homeland Security chief operational executive officer secretary.

By popular request or demand from U.S. citizens a list of possible rogue states have been prepared by the Homeland Security office.
Right-minded citizens are advised caution when travelling to or dealing with these countries.

A list where the rogue states are colourcoded, according to the Homeland Security Department terrorism possibility, can be obtained by sending a selfadressed envelope with $5.65 in stamps to:

Dept. of Homeland Security
C/O The Whitehouse
1. Pennsylvania Ave.
10230 D.C.
