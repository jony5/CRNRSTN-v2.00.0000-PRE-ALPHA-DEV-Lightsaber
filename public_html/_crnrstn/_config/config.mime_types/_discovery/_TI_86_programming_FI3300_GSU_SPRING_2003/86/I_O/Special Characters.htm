I/O Lesson 4

This lesson will demonstrate how to display the special characters contained in
the [CHAR] menu.

 

First, hit [2nd][CHAR] for the special characters menu.

Then, select [MISC], [GREEK], or [INTL] in the sub-menu, and enter the specific
characters you want to use in your program.
