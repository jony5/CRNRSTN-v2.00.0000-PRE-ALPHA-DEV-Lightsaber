I/O Lesson 13

This lesson is similar to the previous lesson, however, it will demonstrate how the round instruction may be used within an Outpt instruction to control the number of decimals displayed in the answer.

:ClLCD
: 6.375  --> A
: 8 / 3 --> B
:Outpt ( 1 , 1 ,"A =   ")
:Outpt ( 1 , 5 , round(A,2))
:Outpt ( 1 , 10 , "INCHES")
:Outpt ( 3 , 1 ,"B = ")
:Outpt ( 3 , 5 , round(B,3))
:Outpt ( 3 , 11 ,"INCHES")
   A = 6.38 INCHES
                                         
   B = 2.667 INCHES
