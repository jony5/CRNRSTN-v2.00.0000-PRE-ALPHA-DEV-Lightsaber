I/O Lesson 7

This lesson is the same as the previous lesson, however, it will use the Outpt instruction instead of the Disp instruction to allow a more precise control over the position of the output on the screen.

 

New Instructions Introduced:

  • Prompt - Allows the user to enter values for the variables contained in the Prompt instruction.


The Outpt instruction allows output to be placed at a specific row and column location on the screen

The Prompt instruction is located in the [PRGM][I / O] menu.

:ClLCD
:Prompt F
:Prompt S , T
:ClLCD
:Outpt( 1 , 1 , "FIRST  =  ")
:Outpt( 1 , 9 , F )
:Outpt( 2 , 1 , "SECOND  =   ")
:Outpt( 2 , 10 , S )
:Outpt( 4 , 1 ,  "THIRD  =  ")
:Outpt( 4 , 9 , T )

Note: To determine the column
position for each variable, count
the number of characters (including spaces) in the quotes, then add 1.
   F =? 4.5
   S =? 6
   T =? 7.38

 

   FIRST = 4.5
   SECOND = 6

   THIRD = 7.38
