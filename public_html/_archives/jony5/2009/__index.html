I'm Jonathan 'J5' Harris, a web professional living and working in Atlanta, GA. I specialize in email program design & message deliverability. In my free time, I jump head first into e-commerce web development, web standards, usability and visual design. This site will eventually serve as the testing ground and showcase of my work.

In 2004 I worked as a freelance designer, web application developer and serial entrepreneur. After the implosion of my start-up company, I entered the world of interactive marketing and advertising when I accepted a UI developer position with an Atlanta based agency, Moxie Interactive.

In 2007 I joined the eCRM team at Moxie and was recently promoted to be the Moxie Interactive eCRM Solutions Engineer. As such, I architect multi-channel data capture and message deliverability systems that support the strategic initiatives of our clients.

Recently, I have started accepting project proposals for small to medium sized businesses, so please feel free to contact me to see if I can help.
