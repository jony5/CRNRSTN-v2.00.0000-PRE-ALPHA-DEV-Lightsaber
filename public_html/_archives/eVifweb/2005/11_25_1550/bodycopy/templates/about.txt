<p><h2>Our Mission</h2></p>

<p>To provide for expanded employment opportunities thereby decreasing unemployment within Fulton County; to provide for an expanded tax base, thereby reducing the tax burden on citizens of Fulton County.</p>

<br>

<p><h2>Our Jurisdiction</h2></p>

<p>The Development Authority of Fulton County (the "Authority") is a state created "public body corporate and politic" pursuant to a 1973 Georgia Law (the Development Authorities Law (O.C.G.A. § 36-62-3)), enacted by a May 16, 1973 resolution of the Board of Commissioners of Fulton County.</p>
